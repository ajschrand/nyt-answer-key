About

This app implements automated solvers for a variety of games in the New York Times collection, including Spelling Bee, Wordle, Connections, Sudoku, and Letter Boxed.

It was created by Adam Schrand as a personal project to practice web development skills and to provide a convenient way to solve these games. The app is not affiliated with the New York Times.

If you have any questions or feedback, feel free to reach out to me by email at adam.schrand@gmail.com.
